About this website


This web-app is a recommender system based web-app that uses your information and requirements to help you choose a car for your needs.

Simply fill in either of the forms available, and the system will produce recommendations that aim to suit your needs.

Each recommendation comes with AI assistance powered by DeepSeek-R1, available to give you more information about the recommendations given.



Which form should I use?


The simple form is recommended for those who don't have much idea about what car they want, but have an idea of what they want to use it for and what they may want from it.

The standard form is recommended for those who have an understanding of what they want in a car, and may have more specific requirements.
